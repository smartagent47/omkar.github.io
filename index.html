About Me

WHO AM I

I'm Omkar Shinde, A Microbiologist Guy. Though My Field is different, I love to Code. For Me Android Development is My Hobby. I Know When i came to Android Development Field I Was a Noob. Now also I am a Noob ..But at that time i used to Make shitty sound mods for Galaxy Y. After sometime when i got a Micromax A116, i started to port roms. I got more interested in Android Development. Time passed away & got more knowledge about Android. Now I have compiled ROMs from source. I have contributed to many projects like CyanogenMod, Paranoid Android, Team Horizon & Many More.

History

In 2012 I got my first phone Galaxy Y. When i got my Galaxy Y i didnt developed anything , i didnt knew what was a custom ROM. In 2013 I got a Micromax A116, i started to port ROMs by replacing libraries & all shit with this phone :P. Later in 2013, I got the Beast & my turning point towards Android Development, the Moto X 1st Generation. I started to read Xda guides, & started compiling ROMs for Moto X. I still remember when I got involved with AOSPA-L Team. My dream came true. It was my dream to join Paranoid Android Legacy Team. As the journey continued, in 2015 I got my first sponsership device from YU, the Yuphoria. The journey still continues.....
